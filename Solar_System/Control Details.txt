Solor System Project:

controls:
's' will change from flying camera to spherical camera
'S' will change from spherical camera to flying camera
in flying camera:
'f' will fly forward
'b' will fly back
't' will fly up
'h' will fly down
'9' will fly left
'0' will fly down
'j' pan left
'k' pan right
'o' pan up
'l' pan down
